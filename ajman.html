Ajman

Ajman,is the fifth-largest city in the UAE, behind Sharjah, Al Ain, Abu Dhabi, and Dubai. It is surrounded by the larger emirate of Sharjah and is situated along the Persian Gulf.

Attractions

alZorah : is an Ajman, United Arab Emirates (UAE) beach neighborhood that lies between Ajman and Hamriyah on the northern boundary of Ajman and Sharjah. The Lux Al Zorah, the Oberoi Beach Resort Al Zorah, and the Al Zorah Golf Club are all part of the Al Zorah mixed-use luxury complex. Al Zorah Nature Reserve, which is home to 60 different bird species including pink flamingos, egrets, and herons, is also located there.

ajmanMuseum : has a number of areas, such as those devoted to archaeology, manuscripts, folk costumes, souvenirs, and more, that provide an intriguing look into Ajman's past. Both Arabic and English annotations are present on the museum exhibits.It is located in a castle from the 18th century that served as the Emirates' top command stronghold and first line of defense. It was once the palace of the monarch before being transformed into the Ajman police station. Two wind towers and two watchtowers were kept while the fort underwent renovation. On the fort's front, a sizable gate and two cannons can be seen.
Ajman Beach : The spotless Ajman beach, which is found in the same-named emirate, features lovely white sand and clear water. For those who enjoy a leisurely holiday, it will be nice during the weekdays when it is less busy. The beach is especially lovely in the evening when a magnificent sunset completes a perfect scene of the surrounding landscape.
