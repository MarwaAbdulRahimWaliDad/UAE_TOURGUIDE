Ras-Alkhaimah

Ras Al Khaimah. After Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Al Ain, and Ajman, it is the sixth-largest city in the United Arab Emirates. A creek divides the city into two sections: Al Nakheel in the east and ancient town in the west.

Attractions

National Museum of Ras-Alkhaimah : is a museum that can be found in the northern United Arab Emirates emirate of Ras al-Khaimah. The museum houses the nation's historical relics and archaeological holdings.
Al Qassimi Palace : This abandoned palace built near the turn of the 21st century is unoccupied and said to be haunted.
Suwaidi Pearls : is situated near Ras Al Khaimah, United Arab Emirates, in the sleepy fishing community of Al Rams, which is perched at the foot of the magnificent Al Hajar mountain range.
